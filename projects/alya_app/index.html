Alya Smart Mirror

The Alya Smart Mirror application serves as an advanced interface for managing the functionalities of Alya Smart Mirror. It offers an array of modular add-ons that can be added or removed at your choice, allowing for a highly personalized user experience. One of the app's defining features is its utilization of facial recognition technology to establish a secure link between the user profile in the app and the corresponding smart mirror.

To facilitate ease of use, the application comes equipped with a step-by-step onboarding guide that aids in setting up the various functionalities. Moreover, the app provides a set of customization options, enabling users to fine-tune the settings of each individual add-on, including its appearance and operational parameters on the mirror itself.
