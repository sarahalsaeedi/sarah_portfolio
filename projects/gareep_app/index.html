Gareep App

Gareeb is a location-based marketplace specifically designed for trading second-hand items. At its core, the app utilizes geolocation services to automatically display items nearest to a user's address, offering a truly localized shopping experience. The platform is divided into various categories, making it easier for users to browse and find items that interest them. One of the standout features is a built-in negotiation system: buyers can propose offers on items, and sellers have the option to accept or reject these offers, streamlining the price-setting process.

Designed to be versatile, Gareeb allows every user to take on the role of both a buyer and a seller. Additional functionalities include the ability to save favorite items for later viewing and a user-rating system to foster a trusted community. To keep users engaged and informed, the app incorporates a notification system that alerts users of new items listed near their location.
