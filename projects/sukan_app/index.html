Dunia Sukan

The Dunia Sukan app serves as a comprehensive digital platform tailored for sports enthusiasts, providing a seamless user experience right from news consumption to event discovery. Offering the latest news and events in the sports world, the app goes a step further by incorporating geolocation features, allowing users to discover sporting events nearest to their location and view these places in detail on Google Maps.

User customization is another core functionality, permitting users to save their favorite sports places and events, as well as pin locations for easy reference. The app enhances user engagement through its promotions section, which showcases select deals and offerings in the sports arena. Security and convenience are also prioritized, with login options available through both Google and Facebook, thereby simplifying the user onboarding process.
