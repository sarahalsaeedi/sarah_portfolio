DineIn Restaurant Management

The DineIn Restaurant App serves as the back-end control center for eateries partnered with the DineIn food delivery service. The app offers a robust platform for menu management, allowing restaurants to dynamically set the availability of items as "new," "out of stock," or "available." Orders placed through the DineIn consumer app are seamlessly integrated into the Restaurant App interface, where delivery times can be managed with precision.

The application segregates orders based on their statusâ€”new, delivered, or on the road providing details of the delivery drivers assigned to each order. Restaurants have the capability to accept new orders and allocate them to available drivers who are online. The app also allows restaurants to indicate their operational capacity by setting their work status as "quiet," "moderate," or "busy" and toggle their opening and closing states.

To boost operational efficiency, the app comes equipped with robust analytics, providing daily, weekly, and monthly reports of orders processed via the DineIn delivery system. Furthermore, the app integrates thermal printer support for invoice generation, with the invoice structure programmable within the application itself.
